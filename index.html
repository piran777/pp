About

Pawmir Painting is a painting and decorating buisness built by Karim Aminullah based in London Ontario. Many of the buildings and houses, around Ontario have been painted or decorated by our services. Pawmir Painting has been actively functioning and succesful for over 30 years. Karim Aminullah built a buisness from the ground up upon arriving in Canada and was able to compete with other businesses in the area almost immediately.
