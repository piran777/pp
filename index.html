Our Work

We take on projects of almost any size. From small sheds to apartment complexes and industrial buildings, we have done it all. We take pride in the many services we provide alongside painting. We can do your flooring, drywall, exterior and interior painting, staining of stairs, paitos and more! Contact us for more information on the services we can provide! Many of the buildings and houses around Ontario have been painted or decorated by Pawmir Painting.

About

Pawmir Painting is a painting and decorating is a buisness built by Karim Aminullah based in London Ontario. Pawmir Painting has been actively functioning and succesful for over 30 years. Karim Aminullah built a buisness from the ground up upon arriving in Canada and was able to compete with other businesses in the area almost immediately. He personally oversees every project and makes sure that everything is done to his standards. He personally works on every project that he takes and makes sure that the work is done on time.
